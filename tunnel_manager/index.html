About Tunnel Manager

I use Putty and Plink on a day to day basis and I needed something reliable, profliable and simple to use, to make quick secure tunnels to various services I have deployed around the net.
Tunnel Manager allows me to do just that.
I can also let other teammates connect to my local computer on our business network and benefit from the tunneling without any client installed!

How it Works

Tunnel Manager provides of a Neutron based, Bootstrap GUI that wraps all the complexity and features of Plink, by Simon Tatham.
It is portable, low specs friendly and very lightweight (~1Mb).

Whether you want to protect an insecure connection, pivot, proxy, or forward some application outside of your corporate network, Tunnel Manager has you covered.
