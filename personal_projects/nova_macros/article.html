Nova Macros (A Stream Deck Clone)

Built for touchscreen controls but also works as a normal app.
Geared towards streamers.

Note: The public version for this tool is a very old version, I have been using it extensively improving it a lot, but I haven't released that version yet. You can contact me for further information on this tool.
